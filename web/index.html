Whale shark

The largest fish alive today. Filter-feeds on plankton and drifts through warm oceans, completely harmless to people.

Greenland shark

A slow, cold-water giant thought to be among the longest-living vertebrates on Earth.

Hammerhead

The hammerhead's uniquely shaped head spreads its senses wide, helping it easily hunt for food along the seafloor.

Shortfin mako

The fastest shark in the sea and a serious open-water traveller.
