CTF Rules

Before you go hunting for flags, please read the ground rules. They keep the challenge fair and the box healthy for everyone.

  1. Don't attempt to DOS the server. This includes brute forcing on the server directly.

  2. Clean up after yourself. If you manage to create files on the server, name them <your name> so you don't confuse other students. (e.g. don't create files called flag.txt — that's just mean.)

  3. Stay on the box. The entire CTF is on this machine. It may be hosted on internal infrastructure, so if you gain access to the CTF machine, do not attempt to pivot.

  4. No AI.Using AI in this lab will not help you learn. The Google overview for questions relating to the systems used is okay.

  5. Have fun!
